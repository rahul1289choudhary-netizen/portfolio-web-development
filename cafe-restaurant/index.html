Our Story

Welcome to The Cozy Cup Café, a warm retreat nestled in the heart of Pune. We believe that every cup of coffee tells a story, and we are here to make yours memorable. From carefully sourced beans to our freshly baked pastry delights, every detail is crafted with love. Come in, find a cozy corner, and let the aroma of roasted coffee beans and sweet pastries take you to a world of absolute comfort.

Whether you're catching up with friends, diving into a good book, or simply watching the city go by, we are your perfect coffee heaven.
